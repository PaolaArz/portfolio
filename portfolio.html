About

I make complex things clear.
And I use AI to do it faster.

I'm the person who spots when something is too complex, too unclear, or too hard to learn, and does something about it.

I have one of those backgrounds that doesn't fit neatly into a box: neuroscience researcher, bioinformatician, technical trainer, theatre director, instructional designer. The common thread is that I've always been the one who bridges the gap between technical complexity and the people who need to understand it.

What I do best is translate complexity into clarity. I break down hard concepts, keep learners genuinely engaged, use visuals to explain what words can't, and build it all into a system that's easy to follow — and faster to produce. At Make, I designed an AI Agents learning path that earned 90%+ satisfaction across thousands of learners, and built a Claude-powered workflow that cut storyboard production from two weeks to two days.

I connect dots across very different fields and bring that perspective to every learning problem I work on. Based in Madrid. Fluent in English, Spanish, Italian, and French.
