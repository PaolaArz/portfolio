SWiRED · Brand Identity

A complete brand identity for a chocolate brand designed for neurodivergent adults, from research and strategy to identity system and packaging.

Concept

A brand for an audience that didn't exist yet in this space

No chocolate brand existed for neurodivergent adults. Products in this space either employed neurodivergent people or were aimed at children. The gap was clear: an adult neurodivergent audience with no brand that understood their daily experience of overstimulation, visual noise, and sensory overload.

SWiRED is built around one insight: neurodivergent people are wired differently, and that is something to celebrate, not fix. The name takes the English word "wired" and adds an "s" to evoke that difference. Playful, direct, without paternalism.

Research

A gap confirmed by benchmarking, a target defined by empathy

The benchmark confirmed the gap: no chocolate brand was targeting neurodivergent adults specifically. Existing chocolate brands with calm aesthetics shared four characteristics, clean legible typography, simple or no isologo, unsaturated flat colours, and maximum negative space. These became the design constraints.

The target was defined through a detailed empathy map: adults 30–45 diagnosed with ADHD, autism, dyspraxia or dyslexia. Employed, culturally engaged, interested in mental health and self-knowledge. They choose brands with calm quiet aesthetics. They reject patronising tones. They are not looking for a product that treats their neurodivergence as a problem to solve.

Design Decisions

Every element answers to the audience

  • Naming SWiRED, from "wired" + an "s" to signal difference. A neologism that is evocative, humorous, and non-clinical to indicate that neurodivergent people are wired differently.
  • Logo Gill Sans, chosen for legibility and reduced visual noise. All caps for clarity, lowercase "i" to signal difference, the W replaced by the brand symbol.
  • Symbol An EEG trace formed by three parallel cables — a direct and playful reference to being wired differently.
  • Colour Two tones from the same family, deep burgundy and soft pink. Flat, calm, unsaturated. No competitor uses this palette.
  • Packaging Rough-textured paper for tactile experience. Embossed elements for self-regulation. Clean composition with maximum negative space. No decorative elements. Designed to stand out through restraint, not noise.
  • Communication Direct and honest. Channels where neurodivergent communities are active, Instagram, YouTube, cinema advertising.
